About Red Beard's Mighty Mouse Kit

This kit includes many menu, including a large collection of pie menus, designed to speed up workflows in Modo. I used to find myself digging through different tabs and menus to find the tools or settings I wanted. I switched to a 12 button mouse years ago and started slowing mapping it for increased productivity. I took many of the best tools in modo and made them easily assessible with a keystroke and a swipe of the mouse. Then I started adding cutsom hot keys for specific tools. I high recommend you map you left and right mouse with tilts to down and up respectively to take full advantage of these specific keybindings. I've also added an "Open with Default App" command to the right click menu, in the shader tree to make these easier to access.

  • About Pie Menus

    • UV Unwrapping Pie Menu

      attr.formPopover {rb_uv_unwrapping:sheet}
    • hit Alt-8

      • This menu will let you quickly preform common unwrapping tasks even when you don't have an UV window open.

      Typical Usecase

      While your building your packaging you can use the Unwrap and Relex command on any polygons or line segements you have selected to quickly build your UV unwraps. You can use the rectangle, fit, and align tools to clean up your unwrap. You can also export your UVs directly from this piemenu.

    • Frame Size Pie Menu

      attr.formPopover {rb_framesize:sheet}
    • hit Alt-9

      • Quickly switch between common frame sizes.

      Typical Usecase

      Quickly switch between common frame sizes. It's great for when you need to render out a quick thumbnail for a preset.
